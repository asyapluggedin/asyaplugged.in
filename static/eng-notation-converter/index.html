the golden rules
⬅️ Too Big
More than 3 digits before the decimal? Move decimal left
0.0015 A → 1.5 mA
➡️ Too Small
Starts with 0.something? Move decimal right
0.942 V → 942 mV
never write 0.something — always convert!
